What is a Pre-Approved Personal Loan

A pre-approved personal loan is an offer provided by a lender to select existing customers with a strong credit profile. Such loan offers are disbursed on the same day or within a few hours of the loan application.

  • ✓ Before offering this facility, banks and NBFCs conduct an initial credit assessment of their customers
    • ✓ Various factors are considered such as credit score,employment status, income, etc.

          • ✓ It is important for you to compare personal loan interest rates from multiple lenders where you have existing accounts, loans, or credit cards.

Who is Eligible for a Pre-Approved Loan in India

Banks and NBFCs offer you a pre-approved personal loan primarily on the basis of your credit profile. Lenders usually consider your account balance and transaction pattern, recent salary credits, existing debt obligations, employment history, loan repayment history, etc., while offering the loan. Key factors for pre-approved loan eligibility include:

  • ✓ Credit Score: A higher credit score, usually 750 or above, is preferred by most lenders to provide pre-approved offers.
    • ✓ Income Stability: Less frequent job changes and consistent salary credits are an important consideration for pre-approved loan offers
      • ✓ Credit History: Lenders prefer applicants with a clean credit history who have repaid or are repaying their loan EMIs and credit card dues on time.
        • ✓ Existing Relationship: Lenders are more likely to offer pre-approved loans to their existing customers with a strong relationship.

How a Pre-Approved Offer Works

Pre-approved offers are just ‘Invitations to Apply’. Receiving a pre-approved offer increases your chances of approval, but it does not guarantee final approval.

  • ✓ When you choose to proceed for a pre-approved offer, your application and supporting documents undergo a detailed review by the lender.
  • ✓ The review may involve additional checks based on the lender’s internal guidelines and policies.
  • ✓ A lender may reject or withdraw your pre-approved offer during the loan application processing if there are any adverse changes in your income, credit score , employment status, or other key financial factors.

Processing Fees & Charges on Pre Approved Personal Loan

  • Do Pre-Approved Loan Offers Hurt Your Credit Score?

  • ✓ Getting pre-approved personal loan offers do not impact your credit score, as you did not apply for the loan.
  • ✓ Once you accept the pre-approved offer, a hard inquiry is initiated which may lower your credit score temporarily.
  • ✓ You should not apply for multiple personal loans at once as it can impact your score.
  • ✓ Compare offers of multiple lenders on the Paisabazaar instant loan app or website and choose the one that suits your financial needs the best.

  • Benefits of a Pre-Approved Personal Loan

  • Higher Approval Chances

  • ✓ Pre-approved loans are offered to individuals who have already passed an initial screening by the lender.
  • ✓ Your chances of approval are generally higher compared to apply for a personal loan from scratch.

  • Faster Processing

  • ✓ Since the lender has already completed the initial evaluation, the processing time for a pre-approved loan is significantly faster.
  • ✓ Once you accept the offer, the final approval and disbursal process is streamlined, helping you access funds quickly.

  • Minimal to Zero Documentation

  • ✓ Lenders usually do not require fresh documentation when processing pre-approved personal loans, as they already have the necessary KYC documents (proof of identity, address, and income) for their existing customers.
  • ✓ This minimizes paperwork and speeds up the loan approval process for those in urgent need of funds.

  • More Favourable Terms

  • ✓ Lenders offering pre-approved loans may provide better terms, such as a lower interest rate, preferred tenure, etc.
  • ✓ You may eventually get loans at lower overall cost of credit.

  • Things to Check Before Accepting a Pre-Approved Offer

  • Compare Multiple Offers: Evaluate offers from different lenders at Paisabazaar to ensure you’re getting the best pre-approved personal loan offer.
  • Consider Loan Terms: Carefully review the loan tenure, EMI amount, and prepayment conditions to ensure they conform to your repayment capacity.
  • Check for Charges: Know about processing fees, prepayment penalties, or late payment charges that could affect the total cost of the loan.

    FAQs

    What is a pre-approved personal loan and how does it work? +
    A pre-approved personal loan is a loan offer made by a lender, usually to existing customers, after analyzing various aspects of their credit profile. The lender assesses your creditworthiness, such as credit score, income, before extending this offer, making the application process faster and more streamlined.
    How Can I Check if I am eligible for a Pre-approved loan? +
    You can check your eligibility for a pre-approved loan by reaching out to your bank or NBFC, or at Paisabazaar to check eligibility. Lenders usually offer pre-approved loans to existing customers with a good credit history.
    Will my credit score be affected if I don't accept a pre-approved loan? +
    Your credit score will not be affected if you choose not to accept a pre-approved loan. Your credit score is affected only if you proceed with the loan, as it would lead to a hard inquiry.
    Are there any hidden charges in pre-approved loans? +
    Pre-approved loans usually have lower processing fees and quicker disbursal. Some lenders may charge additional fees, such as late payment fees, prepayment charges, late payment fees, or other service charges. Borrowers should verify all terms before accepting a pre-approved offer.
    What is the tenure for pre-approved Personal loans? +
    The tenure for pre-approved personal loans usually ranges from 1 to 5 years, though some lenders may offer longer tenures depending on the loan amount and the borrower’s repayment capacity.
    How long does a pre-approved loan offer remain vaild? +
    Unlike regular personal loans, pre-approved loan offers are usually available only for a specific period of time.
    How quickly can I receive funds from a pre-approved loan? +
    Once your pre-approved loan is approved and all necessary formalities are completed, funds can be disbursed quickly, often within the same day or within a few hours. Some lenders, HDFC Bank, offer instant loan disbursal of up to Rs 40 lakh personal loan in 10 seconds for its eligible customers.
    Is a pre-approved personal loan guaranteed? +
    A pre-approved personal loan is not guaranteed, as these offers are just ‘Invitations to Apply’. While a pre-approved offer increases your chances of approval, the final decision depends on a thorough review of your application and supporting documents by the lender. The offer can be withdrawn if there is any adverse change in the applicant’s income or credit score, etc.
